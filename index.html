About Us

We are WambesoExpor! "Papua is the eastern part of the Republic of Indonesia which is rich in natural products, CV. Wambeso is here to introduce Papua's natural wealth through exports with the aim of assisting the Papuan people in developing Papuan natural products, especially Papuan farmer groups. Wambeso itself is the language of the Byak tribe which means the wind that blows in all directions, moving from that meaning we want CV. Wambeso can be one of the pioneers engaged in export."

Our Vision Missions
Our Vision

Introducing Papua through the results of diverse natural wealth and processed by the MSME community to the international world, as well as being a pioneer of services through export activities.

Missions
Our Missions

Three of our missions in our services

Our missions collectively guide our efforts to create a global export company with a strong local services area that not only drives economic growth but also fosters positive social impact and sustainable development.

Promoting the fruits of Papua's natural wealth through cooperation with local Papuan farmers.

Helping to empower Papuans who are engaged in the field of MSMEs.

Educating and providing understanding to the Papuan people that there are many things that can be developed and produced through natural products.
