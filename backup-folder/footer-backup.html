About Us

"Papua is the eastern part of the Republic of Indonesia which is rich in natural products, CV. Wambeso is here to introduce Papua's natural wealth through exports with the aim of assisting the Papuan people in developing Papuan natural products, especially Papuan farmer groups. Wambeso itself is the language of the Byak tribe which means the wind that blows in all directions, moving from that meaning we want CV. Wambeso can be one of the pioneers engaged in export."
